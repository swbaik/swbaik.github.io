Instagram Stories

May 2017 - Ongoing

Before Instagram rolled out the ‘archive’ or ‘highlights’ feature, Stories were short 15 second photos or clips that completely disappeared after 24 hours.

This was especially freeing for me as anything I would publish would simply vanish. Instagram Stories became a casual avenue to post something each day. I felt that I could experiment with shooting or editing techniques without the pressure that comes from more serious projects.

As I continued to create, not only was I able to have fun documenting and presenting my day-to-day life but I was also able to develop a style and audience as well as apply the vertical format videos toward other project collaborations such as Su Bin Hwang's fashion collection titled, “Middle: Either Or Both”.

(last update: 2018)
