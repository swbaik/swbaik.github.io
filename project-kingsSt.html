King's Street Coffee

October 2018

There’s always a story behind a business. Changbin Suh, the owner of King’s Street Coffee, tells his story and the emotional and spiritual passion behind his decision to open up a coffee shop in the middle of Manhattan. I shot, produced and edited this video as an introductory clip for the business.

King’s Street Coffee is located on 30th Street between 6th and 7th Avenue.
