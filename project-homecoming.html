Everyday A Homecoming: Family Life and the Commute

April 2017

Throughout my days as a student, I commuted to school everyday from out of state. This is a feature story that I pitched that explores what the commute means to other students attending The New School.

I helped to conduct the interviews, edited the underlying audio narrative, and directed the shoot. This video is accompanied by an article on The New School Free Press
